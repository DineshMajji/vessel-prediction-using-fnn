Vessel Predictions

Predctive Maintenance

This is used to predict the maintenance of the vessel accurately.Give vessel's current info like lever position, gas turbine and shaft torque etc, we predict and tell you the condition of vessel.We only indicate vessel is in good condition when the output is exactly above the safe condition.The model predicting the output is trained with more than 10,000 cases.Hence it gives you accurate answers.



Excess Energy Consumption

This is used to predict the excess energy which is going to be consumed and is predicted based on the behavioral patterns of the vessel.Inputs from users are taken such as vessel speed,engine rpm and payload etc,the output is the excess energy required. The model predicting is trained using many cases and compares the current behavioral patterns with the basic pattern of energy usgae and calculates the output accurately.
For Operational-Modes enter 1 for Cruising and 2 for Idle.



Route Optimization

This helps you find the Estimated time for you to reach your destination.Give the required locations in latitude and longitude along with average speed of the vessel.
